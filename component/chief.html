He later enrolled with the institute of chartered accountant of Nigeria and qualified as an account in 1996. He set for and passed the examination of the chartered institute of stockbrokers of Nigeria. He is a fellow of the institute and he was elected as a council member in 2011 chief ezenwa ia also a fellow of the certified pension institute of Nigeria and a counsel member of the institute till date. He was worker in various capacities spanning professional accounting practice and financial services.
