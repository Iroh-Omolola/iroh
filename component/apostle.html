Apostle Dr James Grand Okocha

Apostle Dr James Grand Okocha is a seasoned man of God cum business man. He has a doctorate in theology with Zoe Life Theological College Philadelphia USA in 2014 and also elected a bishop of Church Of God In Christ U.S.A that same year. He is a Social commentator, political analyst, author and song writer. He is the Senior pastor of Spring Of Breath Assembly Int'l Lagos, M/D Joevins Global Resources Limited, M/D CEO Juno Industries Int'l Limited, CEO Spirit Music Records, Executive Vice Chairman Tatup Int'l Services Limited, Director China Arab African Business Council, President General Christian Ministers Universal Forum, National Leader Initiative For Better And Brighter Nigeria, Assistant National Coordinator New Nigeria Group, Board Member/ Dean of Studies Light Of Christ Bible University Liberia. He is married with four Children
