Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get started.

Is Tatup International Services Limited (TISL) a registered company?

Yes, we are registered with corporate affairs commission(CAC) with registered number RC-154341 and licensed by United Bank for Africa(UBA) with supper network banking agent.

What line of business is (TISL) into?

We are into software/hardware financial inclusion services?

What makes TISL different from other bank agent?

We have franchise, distributors and agents that sale our cash deposit token to the end users.

What fees do TISL charge on transaction?

TISL charge minimum of ₦ 100 per transaction

How does TISL make money?

Like other banking agent, we make money by collective deposit per transaction in line with government rules. we also make money from license fees from our franchise, distributor and agent registration and other commission services.

What do I need to be an agent or distributor?

On registration the registered person person get a referral link code as an agent, distributors, or franchise. The license fee are not refundable.

Why do you need my bvn?

Your bvn is part of the KYC requirements for transaction purpose.

How do I get the product after registration?

Once you successfully registered, as an agent you can order your product from your distributor, as a distributor you can order your product from franchise while franchise get it directly from Tatup.

How will I get registered if am interested?

You can call our customer care or apply online.

Where's your office?

What should I do if I need help?

The easiest way to reach us is to chat with us or send us a email.
