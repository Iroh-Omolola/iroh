About Us

TATUP International Services, Limited is a registered banking agent that produces a limited scale banking and financial services on behalf of the underserved and unbanked population in Nigeria and beyond. We produce and pioneer Cash Deposit Token in Nigeria. TATUP Cash Deposit Token enables self-cash deposits, and payments outside banking halls.It is available, and useable at anytime, anywhere and every day in Nigeria We are authorized by the traditional banks to extend and to improve financial inclusion, and to ensure a wider customer base in the rural population areas, by providing a number of financial digital and crypto services through our digital platform to the general masses, help businesses scale to a larger level, give people easier access to digital, and digital identities. Our services support various types of transactions such as, voucher-less prepaid top-up, top-up prepaid voucher sales, loan repayments, account balance queries, cash deposits, transfer and bill payments of various types, such as fines, taxes, government fees, utility bills and more. With the use of our product (TATUP Cash Deposit Token), banking halls are decongested, queue reduced, no stress, no time and money wasted, and many more benefits while more transactions become the results. "TATUP Cash Deposit Token" the NUMBER ONE INNOVATION that sends CASH to the bank on air.
